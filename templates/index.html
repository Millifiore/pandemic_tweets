What is BCC ML?

An open-source tool that labels tweets as misinformation, in an effort to educate the public about misinformation on Twitter.

Why is BCC ML needed?

COVID-19 caused widespread concerns globally, as people found factual social media sources have become hard to ascertain.

Our Audience

Our audience is the open source community. Developing an open source tool will allow for further improvements of the tool to be built within the community.

About Demo

We used DistilBert, a pre-trained version of BERT (Bidirectional Encoder Representations from Transformers) to encode tweet data, and Logistic Regression to classify the encoded data. After testing and training our model using our dataset, we received an accuracy rate of 86.4%
