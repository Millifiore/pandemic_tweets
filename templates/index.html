detecting COVID-19 misinformation in text-based social media posts.

Overview

Our team’s intention is to build an open source tool that will accurately label text-based posts as misinformation and point users to correct sources of COVID-19 information.

Why does this matter?

Everyday, posts are shared with the sole intent of informing those within your network. However, these shared posts may contain either falsehoods or outdated information. Social Media Platforms are the public’s fastest source for consuming information, but are they accurately informing the public?

Potential Audience

Our audience is the open source community. Developing an open source tool will allow for further improvements of the tool to be built within the community.
